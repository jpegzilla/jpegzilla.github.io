about me

2019 07 02

my name is jpegzilla. most people just call me jpeg. I am a twenty-year-old web architect and digital artist from georgia. I build things on the internet - experiments, libraries, tools, and websites.

I was the head web developer for a company called bekin, a startup focused on bringing meaningful social connections to the professional networking event scene. my final act was designing and developing their current website.

my main expertise is in front-end web design. however, as you can see from the current bekin website, I also am good with database design, api development, security engineering, typography, and a slew of other things. I am what some call a "unicorn", meaning I am equally skilled in design and development.

all I want is to make things that inspire other people to do better than me, and I'll run towards that goal for the rest of my life.
